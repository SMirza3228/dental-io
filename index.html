Our Doctors

At the heart of DENTAL are our experienced, dedicated dentists who are passionate about delivering the highest quality of care

Dr. Emma Johnson, DDS - General & Cosmetic Dentist

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Johnson is renowned for her gentle touch, meticulous attention to detail, and ability to put even the most anxious patients at ease. A graduate of the prestigious University of Southern California School of Dentistry, she is committed to staying at the forefront of dental advancements and regularly attends continued education courses to expand her skills. Whether you're in need of a routine cleaning, tooth-colored fillings, or a complete smile makeover, Dr. Johnson's expertise and dedication to exceptional care will exceed your expectations

Dr. Michael Patel, DMD - Periodontist & Implant Specialist

With advanced training in the diagnosis and treatment of periodontal disease, as well as the placement of dental implants, Dr. Patel has helped countless patients regain their oral health and confidence. His compassionate chairside manner and unwavering commitment to optimal outcomes make him a trusted partner in comprehensive dental care. Whether you're struggling with gum disease, missing teeth, or need complex restorative procedures, you can count on Dr. Patel to provide the personalized, gentle treatment you deserve.

Dr. Sarah Lee, DDS - Orthodontist

Rounding out our exceptional team of dental professionals is Dr. Sarah Lee, our skilled orthodontist. Dr. Lee earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Michigan before completing her specialty training in orthodontics at the prestigious Mayo Clinic. Known for her meticulous treatment planning and warm, caring chairside manner, Dr. Lee has helped countless patients of all ages achieve beautifully aligned, confident smiles. Whether you're seeking traditional braces, clear aligners, or other orthodontic solutions, she will work closely with you to develop a customized treatment plan to meet your unique needs and aesthetic goals.

Dr. Nicole Ramirez, DMD - Endodontist

Joining our practice is Dr. Nicole Ramirez, a highly skilled endodontist dedicated to providing exceptional root canal therapy and other advanced endodontic treatments. After earning her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from the University of Pennsylvania, Dr. Ramirez completed a prestigious residency in endodontics, honing her expertise in diagnosing and treating complex issues affecting the inner, pulp layer of the tooth. Known for her gentle, patient-focused approach, Dr. Ramirez utilizes the latest endodontic technologies to deliver comfortable, efficient, and successful outcomes for her patients. If you're in need of root canal treatment or other specialized endodontic care, you can trust Dr. Ramirez to restore your oral health and comfort
